## The author

Lance Rossi is the author of the Pratt Study research package. *A full biography is forthcoming.*

## The project

The Pratt Study began in spring 2026 as a conference paper for the National Communication Association's Political Communication Division, examining the Spencer Pratt mayoral campaign's media ecosystem as a generative case for supporter-produced AI political video. The package grew to include a journal-length academic article, a public-facing Substack essay, a practitioner brief for campaign strategists, and an annotated literature review. After the June 2, 2026 primary, the conference paper was revised to incorporate the result — converting the paper's central hedge about virality and votes into a finding. This site is the reference home for all of it.
