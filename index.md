<header class="home-header">
  <h1 class="home-title">Pratt Study</h1>
  <p class="home-tagline">Supporter-generated AI political media and the 2026 Los Angeles mayoral race.</p>
</header>

This site collects the academic and public-facing writing on the 2026 Los Angeles mayoral campaign of Spencer Pratt as a generative case for studying how supporter-generated AI political media is reshaping campaign communication. The package includes a journal-length academic article, an NCA conference paper, a Substack essay, a practitioner brief, and a literature review with annotated sources.
